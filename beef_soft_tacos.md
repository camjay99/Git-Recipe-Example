# Beef soft tacos

## Ingredients
* [seasoned steak](./seasoned_beef.md)
* Soft corn tortillas
* salsa
* lettuce
* guacamole
* Shredded Mexican cheese
* Shredded lettuce
* Sour cream

## Method

Prepare seasoned steak using recipe at [seasoned steak](./seasoned_beef.md), or just reheat remaining steak from previous meal.

Heating the tortillas: For melted cheese, spread tortillas in single layer and sprinkle cheese on each tortilla. 
Heat tortillas for 25 sec in microwave, then cover with foil.

Assemble tacos using all ingredients. 

Serve with beans and/or rice on side.
